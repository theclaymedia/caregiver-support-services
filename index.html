About Us

Born From Compassion, Built for Caregivers

The inspiration behind Caregiver Support Services came from our experience with families facing the difficult decision to place loved ones in long-term care. We saw the emotional strain it caused and the gaps in training for both family members and professional caregivers.

Founded in 1997 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, our mission is to provide caregivers and caregiving organizations with expert, accessible training that equips them with the skills and confidence to deliver exceptional care, wherever they are.

We envision a world where every caregiver, no matter their location or role, has access to the training necessary to provide compassionate care that improves lives.

Retention Solutions

Retain Your Best Caregivers

Caregiver turnover is one of the costliest challenges facing home-care agencies, long-term care communities, and HCBS providers. High turnover disrupts continuity of care, increases burnout, and drives up recruitment costs.

Our training programs address the root causes by preparing caregivers from day one.

✓

Comprehensive Onboarding

Skills-based training that prepares new caregivers for the demands of their role from day one.

✓

Burnout Prevention

Wellness resources and self-care training to sustain caregiver health over the long term.

✓

Progress Tracking

Unified dashboard to monitor caregiver progress, completion records, and certifications.

✓

Regulatory Compliance

Courses designed to meet federal and state training requirements across all 50 states.
